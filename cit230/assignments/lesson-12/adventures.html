Hard Trails in Montana

North Dry Creek

Skill Level: Advance

Milage: 14.4

This is a hard trail to ride. if you are a new or even an intermediate level biker you should stay away from this ride.

Easy Trails in Montana

Mystic Lake

Skill Level: Easy/Intermediate

Milage: 20.4

This trail is not the easiest trail in the world, but it is not hard. if you are new to riding this trail is do able and you will end at the beautiful Mystic Lake.

Ascent/Desent: 1,473/-1,472
